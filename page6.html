The best players in football have incredible close control.

In dribbling, players need to keep the ball close.

They have to guard the ball well.

Why? Because the ball is the most important thing to protect on the field.

The Bible also talks about something else we need to protect.

Our hearts.

Proverbs 4:23: “Above all else, guard your heart, for everything you do flows from it.”

The heart is the core of who you are.

What are you letting in your heart?

How do you control what gets in your heart?

(and what comes out)

The Bible tells us basically, we need to have close control on our hearts.

We have to guard and keep close watch on what we let in.

It’s more than just letting in positivity.

It’s getting what the Bible says in our heart, so we can truly live it out.

Close Control—What’s in your heart?
