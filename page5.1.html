If you know football, you know the term—GIANT KILLERS.

These are the underdogs.

The ones who come out of nowhere and do the impossible.

The ones that maybe others counted out.

A giant killer succeeds because they stick to their plan.

This plan plays to their strengths.

In the same way, God has plans for us too.

Jeremiah 29:11: “For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.”

There is a plan for your life too.

This plan is for you to beat the odds and do amazing things.

God has designed you to be a giant killer.

He works through your strengths and weaknesses.

Even times that it seems like all is lost, He is working it out for His glory and your good.

You may not feel this yet, but it doesn’t mean it’s not true.

Giant Killing—do you know the plans God has for you?
