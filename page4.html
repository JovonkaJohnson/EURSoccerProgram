Goals are a cause for celebration.

The scorer runs towards the stands, the crowd roars, and close behind him…

Is the player who made the assist.

Making the assist is a crucial element to winning the game…And winning at life.

The Bible shows how we can assist others.

Romans 12:15-16: “Rejoice with those who rejoice; mourn with those who mourn. Live in harmony with one another. Do not be proud, but be willing to associate with people of low position. Do not be conceited.”

To assist others in life, we set them up to win.

We listen. We talk. We encourage.

And we celebrate the wins of others.

Because we know that they are made in the image of God.

Assisting means living a life with this in mind.

It’s showing God’s heart and the character of Jesus in all that we do…

Because others are created by God.

Assisting—Who can you “assist” today?
